Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it actually cost? +

Every plan has a £1,000 one-off setup fee. This covers the build and integration. Then you pay your monthly retainer. You also pay for your own tool usage (Twilio, etc.), which is usually £10-20/month. We don't hide costs.

Are there long-term contracts? +

No. We work on a month-to-month basis. If we aren't delivering value, you shouldn't have to pay us. Simple as that.

How long does setup take? +

We typically have your full system live within 7-10 working days from the moment we have access to your tools.

Do I need new software or tools? +

We work with what you already have where possible. If you need any new tools we set them up for you and walk you through the basics. You won't be left figuring anything out on your own.

Will this work for my trade? +

If your business relies on phone calls, enquiries and appointments then yes. We work with plumbers, electricians, roofers, landscapers, builders, cleaners and many other home service businesses across the UK.

How does the AI messaging actually work? +

We use an AI system to write personalised messages on your behalf — using the customer's name, what job they enquired about, and your location. The messages sound like they came from you, not a robot. You approve the tone and style during setup.

What happens if something breaks? +

We monitor and maintain all live workflows. If something stops working we fix it — that's included in your monthly retainer. You don't need to troubleshoot anything yourself.
