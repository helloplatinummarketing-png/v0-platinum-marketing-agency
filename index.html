Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to get started? +

There's a £1,000 one-off setup fee to get your system installed and configured. Then you pay the monthly subscription (Starter £297, Growth £397, or Full System £497). On top of that, you'll pay approximately £10–20/month for the tools themselves (Twilio, API calls, etc.). No long-term contracts — cancel anytime.

How long does setup take? +

Setup typically takes 5–7 working days. We'll integrate with your existing tools, configure your automations, and test everything thoroughly before going live. You'll be up and running within a week.

What if I want to cancel? +

You can cancel anytime with no penalty. We don't believe in long-term contracts. If Platinum isn't delivering value, you're free to leave. That said, most clients stay because the system works.

Will this work for my business type? +

We specialise in UK home service businesses — plumbers, electricians, roofers, landscapers, cleaners, and similar trades. If you take customer enquiries, manage appointments, and do follow-ups, Platinum works for you.

What happens if something breaks? +

You get direct support from our team. We monitor your system, fix issues quickly, and keep everything running smoothly. You're not on your own.

Can I upgrade or downgrade later? +

Absolutely. Start with Starter, upgrade to Growth when you're ready. Or go straight to Full System. You can change your plan anytime, and we'll adjust your billing accordingly.

How do you personalise the AI responses? +

We use Claude API to generate personalised messages based on your business name, service type, and the specific enquiry. Every response feels like it came from you — because it's tailored to your business. No generic templates.
